FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is cold email copywriting as a service?
Cold email copywriting as a service is a done-for-you offering where a specialist writes your outbound email sequences — subject lines, body copy, and follow-ups — instead of your team doing it in-house. At coldemail.club we handle discovery, copy, A/B testing, and unlimited revisions so you can focus on closing the booked calls we generate.
Who is coldemail.club for?
B2B founders, sales teams, outbound agencies, and recruiters running cold outreach who want higher reply rates without burning hours writing email copy themselves.
How long does it take to get a sequence delivered?
Typical turnaround for a first draft of a 4–6 email sequence is 5–7 business days after the discovery call. Revisions are unlimited until you are 100% happy with the copy.
Do you handle email sending and deliverability infrastructure?
We focus on copy, but we help you set up the campaign in your sending tool of choice — Instantly, Smartlead, Lemlist, Apollo, etc. — and review your deliverability setup (SPF / DKIM / DMARC, warmup, domain reputation) during onboarding.
Who owns the copy you write?
You do. Full ownership of every line of copy and every subject line is transferred to you on delivery. Re-use it however you like.
What if my emails do not get replies?
Revisions are unlimited. We A/B test variants against real campaign data, and if a sequence is under-performing we rewrite it until it works.
What does the free email audit include?
A no-pitch review of your current cold email — subject line, hook, value proposition, CTA, and deliverability signals — plus 3 specific rewrites you can use immediately. We send it back as a Loom video within 24 hours.
